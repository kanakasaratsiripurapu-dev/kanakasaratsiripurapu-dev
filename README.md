## About Me

I'm a graduate student at **San Jose State University** pursuing my M.S. in Artificial Intelligence, with hands-on experience building **production-oriented AI and backend systems**. My work spans **agentic AI**, **distributed Big Data (Hadoop / MapReduce)**, **FastAPI backends**, **deep learning research**, and **end-to-end ML pipelines**.

- 🔭 Currently building **SubScout** — an agentic AI platform that scans Gmail inboxes to detect, track, and cancel subscriptions
- 📝 Co-author of a published paper on **HPMR-optimised Hadoop MapReduce** for distributed cancer survival analysis
- 🎯 Open to **Software Engineering · Applied AI · ML Engineer · Backend Engineer** internships and full-time roles
- 🌱 Sharpening skills in **system design**, **iOS / Swift**, and **large-scale distributed systems**
- 📍 Based in San Jose, CA
